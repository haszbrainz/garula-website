Some questions about our product

Garula products are halal because they are made only from natural ingredients that do not come from animals and do not contain ingredients that are prohibited by religion.

Garula is healthier than regular table salt because it contains more natural minerals such as magnesium, potassium and zinc. Garula also has a more complex taste than regular table salt while ordinary table salt is made from sodium chloride, which is a natural mineral contained in sea water.

Garula is rich in minerals, especially magnesium, which can help reduce high blood pressure. Controlling high blood pressure is important for preventing serious complications, such as stroke, heart disease, and kidney failure.
